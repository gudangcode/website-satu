Q. What happen if there are already 12 business ads in the area that I am interested in?

With the limited ad spaces available, we understand that there may be time of insufficient space. We will accommodate your order in first-come first-served basis, and we create a waiting list for the next dry cleaner availability.

Q. Please walk me through the ad design requirement

We provide links for you to download the design format (optional) and to upload your design. We will then review your design and provide update recommendations should there is anything can be improved. You have up to 5 business days to submit a revised design upon our notification date.

Q. When do I have to complete the payment?

Once all the 12 ad designs are ready, your remaining payment will be charged and the designs will go to the print job.

Q. What is your refund policy?

We charge your full payment at the latest possible timeframe before the print job starts. There is no refund for the full payment that you have made. However, in the rare event of your dry cleaner host is unable to distribute the covers entirely, we will refund your payment pro-rata based on the remainder of cover quantity. Events that are considered applicable within this refund policy are:

  1. Bankruptcy, or closure
  2. Moving outside your selected distribution area
  3. Contract termination with Dry Cleaner
